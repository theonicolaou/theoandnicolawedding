Nestled in 12 acres of tranquil Hertfordshire countryside, Sopwell House is a 4 star hotel and the former Georgian country residence of Lord Mountbatten.

Sopwell House is still within easy reach of London, St Pancras and Luton Airport.

Offering a luxury 4 star hotel experience, Sopwell House affords guests full use of The Country Club, which offers some of the finest leisure, health and beauty facilities in the area. Enjoy a leisurely swim in our 14.5 meter swimming pool, workout in the state-of-the art cardiovascular and weight resistance gyms, or unwind in the sauna, steam room or spa bath.
