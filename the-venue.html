Our ceremony and reception will be held at Sopwell House in St Albans. Sopwell House is a 4 star hotel and spa located in 12 acres of beautiful Hertfordshire countryside.

It's easily accessible by car and there is plenty of parking available. For more detailed directions go to http://www.sopwellhouse.co.uk/location.

If you're travelling by train, the nearest station is St Albans City which is a 15 minute journey from King's Cross St Pancras. The station is a 5-10 minute taxi journey from the hotel.
